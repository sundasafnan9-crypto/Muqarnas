About Us

Founded in 2008 by the visionary Mr. Afzal Shah, Muqarnas Constructions derives its name from the intricate threedimensional decorative element that defines classical Islamic architecture. With a commitment to reviving the timeless elegance of traditional Islamic design, we blend artistic heritage with modern innovation to create spaces that resonate with beauty, functionality, and cultural significance. At Muqarnas, we don’t just build structures—we craft your aspirations into reality, delivering what you desire with precision and passion.
