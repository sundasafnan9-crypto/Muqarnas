Meet Our Team

Afzal Shah

Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Afzal Shah is the visionary founder and driving force behind Muqarnas Constructions (Pvt) Ltd. With extensive expertise spanning multiple industries, he has a particular mastery in the construction sector. His ability to anticipate market trends, coupled with his strategic leadership, has shaped the company into a premier name in architectural excellence. Afzal Shah’s unwavering dedication to reviving traditional Islamic architectural styles has provided the firm with a clear road map and long-term vision, making Muqarnas synonymous with innovation and cultural authenticity.

Mohammad Abbas

Senior Project Engineer

Mohammad Abbas, a distinguished graduate from the University of Engineering and Technology, is a seasoned expert with over eight years of hands-on experience in the construction industry. His exceptional technical knowledge and commitment to delivering excellence make him an invaluable asset to Muqarnas Constructions. As Senior Project Engineer, he oversees project execution with a focus on precision, quality, and innovation, ensuring every structure reflects the company's ethos of perfection and timeless design. His leadership on-site ensures that projects are completed seamlessly, exceeding client expectations at every stage.

Abu Huraira

Managing Director

Abu Huraira brings a sophisticated blend of expertise in human resource management and business administration to his role as Managing Director. A graduate of the prestigious National University of Sciences and Technology in Islamabad, he possesses six years of comprehensive experience in overseeing organizational operations and project management. As the overall coordinator for all company projects, Abu Huraira ensures operational efficiency, team cohesion, and client satisfaction. Serving as the focal person for Muqarnas Constructions, he is dedicated to aligning the company’s projects with its mission to create architectural masterpieces.

Uzma Shah

Human Resource Manager

Uzma Shah, a graduate of Karachi University, excels in human resource management with her keen understanding of organizational dynamics and employee relations. As the Human Resource Manager at Muqarnas Constructions, she plays a pivotal role in recruiting and retaining top talent, fostering a collaborative work environment, and aligning the workforce with the company’s strategic objectives. Her meticulous approach and commitment to nurturing a motivated team ensure the smooth functioning of the firm’s operations, laying the foundation for its sustained growth and success.
